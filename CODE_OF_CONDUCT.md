# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want SSHipIt to be a welcoming and respectful project for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on ideas, not personal attacks
- Assume good intent
- Help others learn

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination
- Personal attacks or insults
- Trolling or repeated bad-faith behavior
- Sharing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, lock conversations, or block contributors for violations.

## Reporting

If you see a serious issue, report privately to the maintainer.
